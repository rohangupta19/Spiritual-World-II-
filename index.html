Hope of the Soul
Universe always wants to talk to you ,angel numbers are the repetitive sequence of numbers ( ex. 1212,1313 or 1111,222 ,333)  these numbers are messages from the spiritual universe that offer insight, wisdom, and directionality.

Angel number meanings are always personal; for example ,one person may see "222" as a sign they're on the right track, whereas someone else may experience "222" as a validation of intuition.

When you observe an angel number, my recommendation is to stop, grab a pen and paper (or the notes app on your phone), and write down whatever thoughts, ideas, or impressions come to mind.

That way, you can cultivate a unique relationship with that angel number and, anytime it shows up in the future, you have already defined what that sequence means in your life.
